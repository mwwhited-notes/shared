# Bibliography and Further Reading

## Essential Books

### Analog Computing

**Ulmann, Bernd. *Analog and Hybrid Computer Programming*. De Gruyter, 2020.**
- Modern comprehensive treatment
- Includes THAT Analog Thing examples
- Available in English

**Korn, Granino A. and Korn, Theresa M. *Electronic Analog and Hybrid Computers*. McGraw-Hill, 1964.**
- Classic reference text
- Comprehensive theory and practice
- Historical but still valuable

**Jackson, Albert S. *Analog Computation*. McGraw-Hill, 1960.**
- Excellent introduction
- Strong on mathematical foundations
- Clear explanations

**Truitt, Thomas D. and Rogers, A.E. *Basics of Analog Computers*. John F. Rider Publisher, 1960.**
- Beginner-friendly
- Practical approach
- Good exercises

### Operational Amplifiers

**Jung, Walter G. *Op Amp Applications Handbook*. Analog Devices/Newnes, 2005.**
- Comprehensive modern reference
- Free PDF from Analog Devices website
- Excellent practical information

**Horowitz, Paul and Hill, Winfield. *The Art of Electronics*. 3rd Edition, Cambridge University Press, 2015.**
- Classic electronics text
- Excellent op-amp coverage
- Practical design guidance

**Mancini, Ron. *Op Amps for Everyone*. Texas Instruments, 2002.**
- Free download from TI
- Practical applications
- Good for beginners

### Differential Equations

**Boyce, William E. and DiPrima, Richard C. *Elementary Differential Equations*. Wiley.**
- Standard textbook
- Good for understanding equations solved by analog computers

**Strogatz, Steven H. *Nonlinear Dynamics and Chaos*. Westview Press, 2015.**
- Excellent introduction to chaos
- Physical examples
- Accessible mathematics

### Control Systems

**Ogata, Katsuhiko. *Modern Control Engineering*. Pearson.**
- Standard control theory text
- Transfer functions and block diagrams
- Simulation concepts

## Historical References

**Bush, Vannevar. "The Differential Analyzer." *Journal of the Franklin Institute*, 1931.**
- Original paper on mechanical differential analyzer
- Historical significance

**Lorenz, Edward N. "Deterministic Nonperiodic Flow." *Journal of Atmospheric Sciences*, 1963.**
- Discovery of chaos
- Classic paper
- Used analog computer concepts

**Philbrick, George A. *Applications Manual for Computing Amplifiers*. Philbrick Researches, 1956.**
- Early applications guide
- Historical value
- Practical circuits

## Modern Research

**"Solving ordinary and partial differential equations using an analog computing system based on ultrasonic metasurfaces."** Scientific Reports, 2023.
- Modern analog computing approach
- Wave-based computation

**"Using analog computers in today's largest computational challenges."** Advances in Radio Science, 2021.
- Contemporary applications
- Energy efficiency arguments

**"Hybrid Analog-Digital Solution of Nonlinear Partial Differential Equations."** Columbia University, 2017.
- Modern hybrid approaches
- PDE solutions
