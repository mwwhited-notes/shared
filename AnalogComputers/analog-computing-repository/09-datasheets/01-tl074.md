# TL074 JFET Quad Operational Amplifier

## Overview

The TL074 is a JFET-input quad monolithic operational amplifier containing four independent op-amps. It features high-voltage BJTs and JFETs, providing low input bias current and high input impedance.

## Features

- Low Input Bias Current: 30 pA (typical)
- Low Input Offset Current: 5 pA (typical)
- High Input Impedance: JFET Input Stage
- Low Harmonic Distortion: 0.01% (typical)
- Low Noise: 15 nV/√Hz (typical)
- Wide Common-Mode and Differential Voltage Range
- Internally Frequency Compensated
- Latch-Up Free Operation
- High Slew Rate: 13 V/μs (typical)

## Advantages for Analog Computing

1. **Low Bias Current**: Minimal integrator drift from capacitor discharge
2. **High Input Impedance**: Negligible loading on coefficient pots
3. **Four Op-Amps**: Compact circuits, fewer ICs
4. **High Slew Rate**: Good for fast signals
5. **Low Cost**: Widely available, inexpensive

## Limitations

1. **Moderate Offset Voltage**: Not ideal for precision DC circuits
2. **Offset Drift**: Higher than precision types
3. **Phase Margin**: May need compensation at high gains
