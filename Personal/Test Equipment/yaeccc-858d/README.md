|------|-------------|----------|
| Lead-free desoldering | 380-420°C | Medium-High |

| Heat shrink | 150-200°C | Low |
| Preheating PCB | 150-200°C | Low |

## Safety Notes

- Allow 2-3 minutes cool-down before storing
- Keep nozzle away from flammable materials
- Use appropriate ventilation (flux fumes)
- Don't point at components longer than necessary (board damage)
