## Features

- Point-and-shoot simplicity
- Freeze-frame capture (view only, no storage)
- Hot/cold spot indication
- No training required
- Compact and portable

## Applications

- Finding heat leaks and insulation gaps
- Locating overheating components on PCBs
- HVAC troubleshooting
- Electrical panel inspection
- Motor/bearing temperature checks
- Identifying thermal anomalies

## Limitations

- No image storage/export
- Fixed emissivity (not adjustable)
- Center spot measurement only
- 80x60 resolution (entry-level)
- Discontinued product

## Documentation

- [FLIR TG130 Product Page](https://www.flir.com/products/tg130/)

## Notes

Entry-level thermal imager useful for quick thermal troubleshooting. Good for finding hot components on PCBs, checking power supplies, and identifying overheating ICs. The 80x60 resolution is sufficient for spot-checking but not detailed thermal analysis.
