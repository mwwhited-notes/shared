## Features

- PID temperature control
- Digital temperature display
- Adjustable air flow
- Brushless fan (quiet operation)
- Intelligent induction standby
- ESD-safe design
- Anti-skid base
- Interchangeable nozzles

## Included Nozzles

| Size | Use Case |
|------|----------|
| Small round | SOIC, small QFP |
| Medium round | QFP, PLCC |
| Large round | BGA, large ICs |

## Applications

- SMD soldering and desoldering
- QFP, PLCC, BGA, SOIC rework
- Heat shrink tubing
- Plastic welding/reshaping
- Component removal
- Board preheating

## Recommended Settings

| Task | Temperature | Air Flow |
|------|-------------|----------|
| Lead-free desoldering | 380-420°C | Medium-High |
| Leaded desoldering | 320-360°C | Medium |
| Heat shrink | 150-200°C | Low |
| Preheating PCB | 150-200°C | Low |

## Safety Notes

- Allow 2-3 minutes cool-down before storing
- Keep nozzle away from flammable materials
- Use appropriate ventilation (flux fumes)
- Don't point at components longer than necessary (board damage)
