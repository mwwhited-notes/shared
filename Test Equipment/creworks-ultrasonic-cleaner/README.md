## Features

- Dual slot design for multiple items
- Integrated heater for improved cleaning
- Digital timer control
- Ultrasonic cavitation removes contaminants without scrubbing

## Applications

- PCB cleaning (flux removal)
- Component cleaning
- Tool cleaning
- Parts degreasing
- Vintage electronics restoration
- Watch and jewelry cleaning
- Carburetor and small parts cleaning

## Cleaning Solutions

| Application | Solution |
|-------------|----------|
| PCB flux removal | IPA or dedicated flux remover |
| General degreasing | Simple Green, dish soap |
| Heavy grease | Purple Power, degreaser |
| Delicate items | Distilled water + mild detergent |

## Safety Notes

- Use appropriate cleaning solutions (check material compatibility)
- Wear hearing protection during extended use
- Ensure adequate ventilation
- Do not operate empty
- Allow parts to dry completely before powered use
