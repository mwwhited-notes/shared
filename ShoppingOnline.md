- **Red Flags:**
  - Deceptive product photos hiding damage
  - Stickers applied to product (possibly hiding defects)
  - Zero protective packaging
  - Improvised shipping container
- **Would Buy Again:** No
- **Notes:** While the unit works, the deceptive photos and complete disregard for packaging make this seller untrustworthy. Avoid.
