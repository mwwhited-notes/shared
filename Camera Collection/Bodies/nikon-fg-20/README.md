## Features

- Compact and lightweight 35mm SLR
- Aperture-priority auto and full manual modes
- M90 mechanical shutter backup (no battery required)
- Audible low-light warning below 1/30s
- LED exposure display in viewfinder
- Released in 1984

## Compatible Lenses

- Nikon AI and AI-S lenses (full metering)
- Nikon Series E lenses
- Non-AI lenses (stop-down metering only)
- Pre-AI lenses require conversion
